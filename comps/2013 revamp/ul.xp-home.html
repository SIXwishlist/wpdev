Urban Legend web design Whangarei is based in Northland, New Zealand, and designs, codes, updates, and develops web sites for international and local clients.

Web Design

We aim to build web sites that are well-designed, yet clean, and fast-loading. In other words, sites which are built for end users – simple enough to understand, navigate and enjoy, but designed well enough for those users to enjoy. We like to think this site, and most of those in our portfolio, meet those standards.

Web development

By web development we mean working with, or creating, an ‘engine’ which drives dynamic websites. The pre-packaged versions of these are commonly known as Content Management Systems, and can allow website owners to edit content, list and sell products, create an image gallery, run a subscription service, and more. All without knowing anything about how to web design. We also produce our own simple purpose-built ‘engines’.
